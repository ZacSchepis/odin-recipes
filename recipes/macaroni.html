Macaroni

Macaroni and cheese is a simple-to-make dish

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Elbow Noodles

Instructions

  1. Boil water
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Create a roux by melting equal parts butter to flour, then add Milk
  4. Mix in the cheese, slowly incorporating it into the sauce
  5. Combine
