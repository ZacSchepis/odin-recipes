Tacos

Finished products of tacos

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Cheese
  • Beans
  • Flour Tortillas
  • Black Beans
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Diced onion
  • Diced tomatoes

Steps

  • Brown beef in a pan
  • Once browned, add the onion, and tomatoes to the beef
  • Mix in taco seasoning with some water
  • Let it reduce for a while to remove excess water
